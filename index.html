Hello There!! I'm Bhargav, currently finishing up my undergraduate study at GITAM University with a Computer Science & Engineering major. Here, apart from doing excessive competitive programming, I spent two years springing across different CS domains like cybersecurity, cloud, app development, and IoT. Spending the remaining two years dating deep learning. Looking ahead, I want to increase my knowledge, experience, and contribution to the world through graduate study.

Research Experience

Not all of my research endeavors resulted in papers. Those which did end up getting published can be found here. Those with great reasons to exist but had better reasons not to are as follows. These works ended up becoming projects which are available on my github.

Absolute Entropy from Quantum origins. In this paper, I tried to prove the existence of absolute randomness using a simple quantum circuit. But the proof required profound knowledge of theoretical physics.

Music Signature. Here, I used a neural net with music theory logic to capture the signature style of any musician. But this was better off as a project than paper and also required a lot of data that was copyrighted.

Hairstyle Generator using GANNs. Everything went right until the results part. The model requires changes from a more experienced person in the GANN industry. It also needs to be trained with tuned hyper clusters for faster results.

Haar cascade for ears. The architecture turned out to be too reductive for it to be a novel finding. Although, finding the haar cascade led to interesting data from analyzing human facial features.

Effect of climate change. This project is too parochial and focused on my home. The data set I am creating here can help develop CV algorithms that can understand factors at play between the lens and the object.

Why is my house collecting so much dust? This was a fun experiment that I conducted at my home with many IoT sensors. I wanted to find out why summer was causing so much dust on my windows after I woke up. The result, however, is hilarious. Due to the hot weather of Visakhapatnam, people tend to turn on their AC a lot, especially at night. This caused dust to settle on my windows when I woke up.
